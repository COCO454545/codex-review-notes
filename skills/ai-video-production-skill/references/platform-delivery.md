# Platform Delivery

Use this when platform, export package, captions, cover, or publishing readiness matters.

## Defaults

| Platform | Ratio | Resolution | FPS | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Douyin / TikTok / Shorts / Video Account | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | 30 | Strong first screen, readable phone text, captions safe from bottom UI |
| Bilibili / YouTube long-form | 16:9 | 1920x1080 | 30 | Slower pacing, section titles, clearer tutorial continuity |
| Square social post | 1:1 | 1080x1080 | 30 | Larger text, fewer columns |

## Vertical Safe Zones

For 1080x1920:

- Keep important text at least 54px from left/right.
- Avoid critical captions in the bottom 220px when publishing to short-video apps.
- Keep title and key proof visible without relying on tiny UI text.

## Publish Package

When requested, produce:

- Final MP4.
- 3-5 preview frames.
- Suggested cover frame or cover image.
- 3 title options.
- 1 short description.
- Captions/subtitle file when the workflow generated timed subtitles.
- Notes on source/reference usage.

## Acceptance

Do not call the package ready if:

- The opening frame does not communicate the topic.
- Phone-size text is unreadable.
- The voice track is missing when narration was requested.
- The cover/title promise is stronger than the actual content.
